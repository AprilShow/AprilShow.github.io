The Pollock Private Foundation (in formation)

A private foundation in the process of establishment, dedicated to supporting charitable organizations that embody enduring values of education, creativity, and cultural stewardship.

Initial Recipients

  • Besant Hill School of Happy Valley — for its commitment to education as empowerment and the cultivation of independent thought.
  • UCSB Pollock Theater — established in 1966 in California through the Joseph & Helene Pollock Foundation, affirming the role of the arts as cultural memory.

Plaque at the UCSB Pollock Theater

Why these values?

These organizations represent archetypical values that guide the Foundation: education as a means of personal liberation, art as a vessel of cultural identity, and stewardship as a duty to future generations. By supporting them, the Foundation affirms that progress is measured not only in achievement, but in preservation and care.
